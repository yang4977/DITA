Configuring Manual Mode Settings
Manual mode is the basic setting for all photographers and can be adjusted to accommodate various lighting conditions. You should adjust Manual mode settings accordingly to the amount of light present in a dark or bright environment. This allows for consistency within you photographs as exposure settings stay intact throughout lighting changes in your environment.
Configuring Basic Exposure Settings
Manual mode consists of three beasic exposure settings: ISO, Aperture, and Shutter Speed
Configuring the Camera Lens
The camera lens is the most important tool, which brings light to a fixed focal point. Various lenses have different capabilities in creating quality photographs. Likewise, the camera lens you choose sets specific limitations on your exposure settings. You have the ability to invest in the best lenses to showcase your photography skills!
